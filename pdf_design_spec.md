# PDF Slide Design Specification (Paul Stokes SA Example)

## General Design Rules
- Background: Pure black (#000000)
- Primary text: White (#FFFFFF)
- Accent color: Aqua (#00EAD3) - used sparingly for subtitles, table headers, graph bars
- Highlight color: Orange (#f36710) - used for key numbers (savings, payback)
- Fonts: NextSphere for headings (bold, uppercase), GeneralSans for body text

## Slide Structure (16 slides total)

### Slide 1: Cover Page
- Logo top-left with "ELITE SMART ENERGY SOLUTIONS" text in aqua
- Large title in white NextSphere font: "IN-DEPTH BILL ANALYSIS & SOLAR BATTERY PROPOSAL"
- Customer name and address at bottom with orange left border accent
- Right side: Tech illustration with solar panels and aqua glow effects

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
- Title: "EXECUTIVE SUMMARY" (white, NextSphere)
- Subtitle: "Your Energy Transformation at a Glance" (aqua)
- Four key metrics in a row with vertical dividers:
  - Current Annual Bill (white number)
  - Projected Annual Bill (white number)
  - Total Annual Savings (aqua number)
  - System Payback (orange number with "Years")
- Description paragraph with aqua left border

### Slide 3: Current Bill Analysis
- Title: "CURRENT BILL ANALYSIS" (white)
- Subtitle: "Billing Period: [dates]" (aqua)
- Table with columns: COMPONENT (aqua), DETAILS (aqua), AMOUNT (aqua)
- Rows: General Usage, Daily Supply Charge, Total Charges, Solar Feed-in Credit
- NET BILL row highlighted in aqua
- KEY INSIGHT box on right with orange left border

### Slide 4: Monthly Usage Analysis
- Title: "MONTHLY USAGE ANALYSIS" (white)
- Subtitle: "Your Energy Consumption Pattern" (aqua)
- Bar chart comparing: Your Usage (aqua bar), Small Home, Medium Home, Large Home (gray bars)
- Stats on right: Your Daily Usage (aqua value), household comparisons (white values)
- EFFICIENCY INSIGHT box with orange left border

### Slide 5: Yearly Cost Projection
- Title: "YEARLY COST PROJECTION" (white)
- Subtitle: "Annual Analysis & 25-Year Outlook" (aqua)
- Table: PERIOD, GRID USAGE, SOLAR EXPORT, NET COST
- KEY FINDING box with aqua left border
- Line chart: "25-YEAR CUMULATIVE FINANCIAL OUTLOOK"
  - Orange line: Cumulative Bill Cost (Current)
  - Aqua line: Cumulative Total Benefit (Proposed)

### Slide 6: Proposed System
- Title: "PROPOSED SYSTEM" (white)
- Subtitle: "Optimized for Your Energy Profile" (aqua)
- System specs in cards: Solar System, Battery Storage, Total Investment
- Key benefits list with aqua checkmarks

### Slide 7: VPP Comparison
- Title: "VPP PROVIDER COMPARISON" (white)
- Subtitle: "Virtual Power Plant Options" (aqua)
- Table comparing providers with columns for rates, terms, annual income
- Best option highlighted

### Slide 8: Financial Summary
- Title: "FINANCIAL SUMMARY" (white)
- Breakdown of costs, savings, and ROI
- Payback period prominently displayed in orange

### Slide 9-16: Additional slides for EV, rebates, next steps, contact info

## Chart Styling
- Bar charts: Aqua for customer data, gray for comparisons
- Line charts: Aqua for proposed/benefits, Orange for current/costs
- Dark background with subtle grid lines
- Rounded corners on chart containers

## Table Styling
- Headers in aqua uppercase
- Aqua underline below headers
- White text for data
- Key totals in aqua or orange

## Insight Boxes
- Dark background (#1a1a1a)
- Left border: Aqua for insights, Orange for key findings
- Title in aqua/orange uppercase
- Body text in white
